Problem

Food insecurity prevents thousands of adults and children from reaching their full potential. Without consistent meals on their plates, families can suffer from adverse health effects such as malnutrition, impacting other areas of life such as education and work. But this isn't an issue that can't be resolved.

Mission Statement

Vision Statement

To reduce food insecurity in the Pittsburgh area and educate residents on how to reduce food wastage
